Extensions to Schema.org for structured, semantic & executable research documents

As we near a v1.0 release, we presented and asked for feedback on, the current state of Stencila Schema - https://github.com/stencila/schema . For those not familiar, the project is a set of extensions for schema.org in support of structured, semantic, and executable research documents.

Chaired by Nokome Bentley, CEO of Stencila

Panelists: Thad Guidry, Arfon Smith, Vanessa Sochat, Dan Katz, Paul Shannon
